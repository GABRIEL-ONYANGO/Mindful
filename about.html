Our Purpose

MindfulMate was developed to provide online specialist therapy services for teenagers experiencing anxiety, stress, grief, trauma, loss, bereavement, and other mental issues. While the aim is to provide a safe space for teenagers battling with mental issues, our online therapy team has extensive experience in treating a broad range of mental health issues.

Our mission is to help teenagers in our urban community and beyond receive the tools and support they need to heal, rebuild, and thrive in every area of their lives.

Our Story

MindfulMate was developed as safe space to provide specialized counseling for teenagers experiencing mental issues. Our complimentary online team of therapists have first-hand knowledge of mental health issues and know exactly how hard it is to rebuild your life after experiencing mental trauma. They also knows what it’s like to feel unnoticed and unloved. Their commitment to your story is proof that healing creates hope.

There is light at the end of the tunnel when you are ready to find it. Our online team is here to listen to your story and help you take the first steps to recovery.

Our Approach

We take an integrative and holistic approach to therapy. While talk therapy is the cornerstone of what we do, we integrate a variety of services that promote physical, spiritual, and emotional healing. Our personalized healing approach takes into account the full picture of your well-being.

Through movement, music, sound, art, and gentle touch, and more, we’ll help you discover therapeutic practices that prepare your body and mind for healing.

Our Online Therapists

A great therapist-client relationship creates a foundation for your healing. We’re proud to be home to some of the top team of mental health therapy specialists. Our clinicians are well-versed in helping you heal from a wide range of mental health issues, and each therapist on our team specializes in a specific niche within mental wellness therapy.

Whether you want to explore art therapy, attend a group therapy session for bereaved teenagers, or get post-adoption support, we provide specialized services for your unique journey.
